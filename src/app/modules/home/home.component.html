About Sanctions Workflow

There are 4 main work stream involved smart territory; broad/mulitple territory; controlled goods territory; payment process and respective forms from each area will be processed in this sanctions workflow online application. This will undergo a process flow involving submission(business/operation team), approvals(sacntions/bespoke team), auto aprroval for SMART sanctions(automated system process), maintenace of audit log information, management of master information(admin team) and generation of management information reports.

This online application will be used globally by 26+ counries with provision for multi-language capability.
